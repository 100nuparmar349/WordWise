The Best Homemade Pizza Recipe

Pizza is one of the most beloved dishes in the world, and nothing beats a homemade pizza! With fresh ingredients and a perfect crust, you can enjoy an authentic taste right from your own kitchen.

This recipe will guide you through creating the perfect pizza from scratch. Whether you prefer a thin, crispy base or a thick, chewy crust, this method can be easily adapted to suit your taste. Let’s dive in!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ½ cups of flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup warm water
  • 1 package of active dry yeast
  • Your favorite toppings (cheese, pepperoni, veggies, etc.)

Instructions:

1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). Grease a pizza pan or baking sheet with olive oil.

2. In a small bowl, dissolve yeast and sugar in warm water. Let it sit for about 10 minutes until frothy.

3. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt. Add yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.

4. Knead the dough on a floured surface for about 5-7 minutes, until smooth and elastic.

5. Roll out the dough to your desired thickness and place it on the prepared pan.

6. Add your toppings and bake for 12-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown.
